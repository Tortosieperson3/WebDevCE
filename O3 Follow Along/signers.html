Who were the Signers?

Famous Signers of the Declaration of Independence

Most Famous Signers

  • Thomas Jefferson (Virginia)
    Principal author of the Declaration. Later became the 3rd President of the United States.
  • John Adams (Massachusetts)
    Advocate for independence. Later became the 2nd President of the United States.
  • Benjamin Franklin (Pennsylvania)
    Renowned inventor and diplomat. Helped edit the Declaration.
  • John Hancock (Massachusetts)
    President of the Continental Congress. Famous for his large, bold signature.
  • Samuel Adams (Massachusetts)
    Revolutionary leader and cousin of John Adams.

Other Notable Signers

  • Richard Henry Lee (Virginia)
    Proposed the original resolution for independence.
  • Roger Sherman (Connecticut)
    Only person to sign all four major U.S. founding documents.
  • Charles Carroll (Maryland)
    Only Catholic signer. Longest-living signer (died in 1832).
